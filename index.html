ABOUT LMKC Diglossia Inc.

LMKC Diglossia Incorporated is an Audit and Financial Consultancy firm and we have been in operation since 2010. The firm is 100% black owned and is led by Lubabalo Mpepanduku who a qualified chartered accountant and a registered auditor. We have a full staff complement of 10 people (6 fulltime and 4 temporary) and are looking to increase our headcount significantly in the near future. The firm is registered with both the South African Institute of Chartered Accountants (SAICA) as well as the Independent Regulatory Board for Auditors (IRBA). We offer a full bouquet of financial services – we are one stop shop. Over the years we have delivered quality service to all our clients in both the private and public sector. Our private sector clients vary in size and range from clients with annual turnovers of R200m to micro-businesses who turnover less that R10m per annum. We have serviced various public sector clients from departments to municipality entities.
